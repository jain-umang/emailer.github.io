How it all began?

Our journey began more than 5 years ago in Mumbai over a late evening dinner with friends. Since most of my friends came from the financial world, the discussion naturally got inclined towards it.

 

To give a little background, myself, CEO and Co-Founder of Tavaga, have been an Equity Research analyst for more than a decade and was often perplexed at how autocratic the investment advisory space is. I was frustrated that there was hardly any formal and affordable channel for a common man to deploy his hard-earned money. The discussion led from one thing to another, and the idea was born! Low-cost investing + technology as the backbone + best-in-class analysis - for retail investors.

Voila!

We named it Tavaga – a Sanskrit word for bull, which denotes wealth and prosperity in the investing parlance. I believe there is never an ideal time to take a risk in your career, especially if you have a wife and a toddler at home. Fortunately, I have always had support from my family and friends for whatever decision I made.

The initial hiccups

We underwent a lot of discomfort, uneasiness, and struggles to start with this idea and build it each day.Despite growth in the global theme of passive investing, the real challenge lay in chalking out a roadmap, within the regulatory boundaries.We dabbled with multiple SEBI-regulated licenses from a Stockbroker to Mutual Fund Distributor but what worked out for our unique model, after multiple reiterations, was an Investment Adviser or what SEBI defines as Registered Investment Adviser (RIA).What makes Tavaga’s proposition unique?

Tavaga is India’s first registered Robo-Advisory platform that predominantly facilitates investments through Exchange Traded Funds or ETFs. We deploy sophisticated and proven techniques of Index Investing, Auto Rebalancing, and Glide Path Investing to ensure that our invested money has opportunities to grow faster with optimised risks.

We want to make investing accessible and easy for people who need it the most! Investors get to pick their own SIP amount, their financial goals and their own timelines.

Through Tavaga, we want to address three key challenges retail investors face:

a) Information overload
b) Difficulty in understanding complex financial products
c) Trap of mis-selling

How has the journey been so far?

Tavaga has been growing in leaps and bounds ever since. From just the two of us working out of my study, we have grown to an enthusiastic bunch of 15 working from across the country. The platform has been attracting consumers from different demographics. The pandemic-induced digital penetration accelerated the growth of the fintech ecosystem. More and more people have started taking savings and financial health seriously and Tavaga has been witnessing these changing lifestyle habits from the front lines.

Moreover, we have been collaborating with financial intermediaries and couldn’t be prouder to partner with leading Stockbrokers in the country to cultivate investing habits and make financial literacy a household discussion.Not to forget the incredible team and culture that we have built. Working at Tavaga is not a job, it’s a mission and you will hear more about it in the coming editions
